Cube

Get the most out of your data

  • import

    Get your CSV, XML, JSON any other file where you keep your data, and import it into one common place.

  • manage

    View your data organized. Filter, search, modify and get valuable information from it.

  • extend

    Use CoffeeScript and Style to extend the Cube's look and functionality to suit your taste.

Get to know your data

How much do you know about your data? With the use of solr faceted results, the cube shows you counts of all your items and their properties.

Detailed view

Each of your things gets a detailed view where you can see all its properties and use them to filter the collection, send emails or edit one by one.

Filter and Search

Use the same facets as before but this time to filter the data that you want to see.

Manage your team

Use the Cube special team management features to help you keep everyone together while reducing time reaching each other.

Simple API

Once you get your data in the cube, you can get it in many ways: json, xml, csv. Either the whole collection or just a part of it, all with a simple url.

Keep it together

The Cube can handle as many sets of data as you want. In the future, you will be able to relate one set ot another, so that your projects, teams and services, for example, can relate to each other.

NodeJs, BackboneJs and Apache's Solr blazing fast search platform are some of the open source building blocks of the cube.

Open Source

The cube has already become one of our daily used apps here at Zalando, and we want to share it with you. We have now made it open source and pushed it to our GitHub repository. You can clone it or fork it, extend it and send you your pull requests!. But for friendly installation instructions, please visit the install documentation.
